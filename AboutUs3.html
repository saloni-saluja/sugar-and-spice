Our Story

A popular Ontario-based retail bakery, Sugar & Spice specializes in whimsical cupcakes, cakes and other nostalgic baked goods made fresh daily.Founded by best friends Saloni, Ekjot and Manjul in 2018, Sugar & Spice bakes their products from scratch and uses preservative-free, basic ingredients, offering customers fresh batches of yummy goodness daily.

It was also the Sugar & Spice Bakery featured on the hit reality TV Show called, ‘The Yum Yum Show’ on the S Network. The TV Show airs in 90 countries worldwide, including the USA, Asia, and Latin America. The Sugar & Spice brand stands for fun, nostalgia, and celebration!
